Dune Weaver turns your phone into a full remote for your kinetic sand table running Dune Weaver Firmware. It talks directly to the table over your Wi-Fi — no cloud, no account, and no separate server to keep running. Nothing leaves your network.

BROWSE & RUN
- Explore a library of around 1000 patterns and run any of them with a tap.
- Optional pre-run clear: adaptive, from the center, from the perimeter, or sideways.

YOUR OWN PATTERNS
- Import your own .thr files at full resolution and push them straight to the table's SD card.
- 100 built-in patterns ship with preview thumbnails; imported patterns get previews generated right on your device.

PLAYLISTS
- Create, edit, reorder, loop and shuffle playlists.
- Pause between patterns, clear, and auto-home options.

FULL CONTROL
- Home, stop, pause and resume, and adjust speed.
- Live status with a polar progress trace and real-time ball position.

LED RING
- Effect, palette and color pickers, brightness and speed.
- Run and idle overrides plus a ball-tracker effect, driven by the table's on-board LED ring.

MORE
- Quiet hours ("Still Sands") to pause overnight.
- Auto-play on boot, multi-table support with a quick switcher, custom name and logo, and light or dark themes.

The phone and table must be on the same Wi-Fi network. Dune Weaver is open source (GPL-3.0).
